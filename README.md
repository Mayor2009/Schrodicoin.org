  <h1>Schrodicoin Whitepaper</h1>

  <h2>Introduction</h2>
  <p>
    Schrodicoin is a revolutionary, community-driven cryptocurrency designed to empower and uplift underserved communities around the world. Born from the principles of quantum uncertainty and collective action—inspired by Schrödinger’s cat—we exist in a state of potential until the community observes and acts.
  </p>
  <p>
    Our mission is simple yet profound: <strong>to be a community that helps communities.</strong> By leveraging blockchain technology, decentralized governance, and a portion of transaction fees dedicated to charitable initiatives, Schrodicoin turns everyday transactions into tangible support for those in need.
  </p>

  <h2>Key Focus Areas</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Education:</strong> Providing scholarships to foster opportunity.</li>
    <li><strong>Basic Needs:</strong> Assisting with groceries and bill payments.</li>
    <li><strong>Family Support:</strong> Covering childcare costs to enable parents to thrive.</li>
    <li><strong>Environmental Impact:</strong> Funding clean-up crews to remove junk and restore neighborhoods.</li>
    <li><strong>Community-Voted Initiatives:</strong> Disaster relief, mental health support, vocational training, and more.</li>
  </ul>

  <p class="highlight">
    As the token grows in value through adoption, so does the impact of our giving.
  </p>

  <ol>
    <li><strong>Acquire SCHRO:</strong> Buy on DEXs or earn via community tasks.</li>
    <li><strong>Stake & Govern:</strong> Stake tokens to vote in the DAO.</li>
    <li><strong>Nominate Causes:</strong> Submit initiatives and vote on them.</li>
